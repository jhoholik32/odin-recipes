Peanut Noodles

This is my favorite comfort food to eat. These peanut noodles are easy to make. Its quick fast and easy to make. Follow the recipe to enjoy a nice comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Boiled Water
  • Minced Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Peanut Butter
  • Rice Wine Vinger
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Chili Garlic Sauce
  • Lime Juice
  • Warm water

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Bring 3 cups of water to a boil
  2. Step 2: Add in your own favorite noodle
  3. Step 3: In a bowl Mix the following: 1/2 cup Peanut Butter, 2 Tbs. Soy Sauce, 1 Tbsp Rice Vinger 2 Tbsp Brown Suger, 2 tsp Chili Garlic Sauce, 1 Tbsp Lime Juice, 3 Tbsp Minced Garlic, 1 Tbsp of Ginger, and 2 to 4 cups of warm water for desired consistancy
  4. Step 4: when noodles are el dente (to the tooth) Strain them in a calender
  5. Step 5: Turn off heat and add noodles back into pan
  6. Step 6: add the sauce to noodle and mix
  7. Last step is to put in bowl and enjoy!!!
