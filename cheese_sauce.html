Cheese Sauce

>Cheese Sauce

This recipe goes great for parties. You can use it as a dip. Top your nachos or even top a chili dog. This cheese sauce is easy to make and doesnt require much Ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of Rotel green chili and diced tomatos
  • 1 package of Velveeta cheese (32oz)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Open the 2 cans of Rotel
  2. Step 2: Dump the Rotel into a pot
  3. Step 3: Cut the Velveeta Cheese into cubes
  4. Step 4: Put the cubes into pot
  5. Step 6: Cook over medium heat for 8 min and stir occasionally
